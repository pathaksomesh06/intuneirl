<p>Streamlining the device enrolment process is crucial for administrators and enterprises implementing BYOD policies. With&nbsp;Microsoft Intune's web-based device enrolment for iOS/iPadOS, setting up and managing devices becomes more efficient than ever. By eliminating the need for the Company Portal app, this solution offers a faster enrollment experience. As an MDM admin, you can now easily enrol personal devices in Microsoft Intune without requiring users to install additional apps. This not only saves time but also enhances productivity by simplifying the enrollment process. This eliminates the need for complex manual configurations and reduces the time and effort required for device enrolment. By leveraging&nbsp;Entra ID authentication, just-in-time registration ensures a secure and efficient enrolment process.</p><p>For steps, see&nbsp;<a href="__GHOST_URL__/just-in-time-registration-for-ios-ipados/" rel="noreferrer">Set up JIT registration in Intune</a>.
